Welcome to Bird Page!

Physical Characteristics: Birds are warm-blooded vertebrates with feathers, beaks, and hollow bones, allowing for flight.

Species Diversity: There are approximately 10,000 species of birds worldwide, each adapted to various environments.

Behavior: Birds exhibit diverse behaviors such as nesting, migrating, and communicating through songs and calls.

Diet: Birds are omnivores, feeding on seeds, fruits, insects, and even small animals, depending on the species.

Habitat: Birds inhabit a variety of environments, from forests and wetlands to urban areas and deserts.
