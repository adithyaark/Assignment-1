Welcome to Rabbit Page!

Physical Characteristics:Rabbits are small mammals with distinctive long ears, fluffy short tails, and whiskers. They belong to the family Leporidae and exhibit various fur colors and patterns

Species Diversity:: There are approximately 29 species of rabbits worldwide, each adapted to different habitats, from meadows to forests.

Behavior: Known for their social nature, rabbits often live in groups and exhibit behaviors such as grooming and playing. They are crepuscular, meaning they are most active during dawn and dusk

Diet: Rabbits are herbivores, primarily eating grasses, vegetables, and hay. A balanced diet is crucial for their digestive health

Habitat: Wild rabbits construct burrows in the ground, providing shelter from predators and harsh weather conditions
