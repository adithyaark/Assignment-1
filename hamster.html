Welcome to Hamster Page!

Physical Characteristics: Hamsters are small, nocturnal rodents with short tails and pouches in their cheeks.

Species Diversity: There are several species of hamsters, including Syrian, dwarf, and Roborovski hamsters.

Behavior: Hamsters are known for their burrowing habits and can be very playful and curious.

Diet: They primarily eat seeds, fruits, and vegetables, but need a balanced diet for health.

Habitat: In the wild, hamsters typically inhabit grasslands and deserts, digging extensive burrows.
